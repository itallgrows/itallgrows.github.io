What we're about

It All Grows was created by Alzainah. It started as an idea to see if one can grow vegetables and fruits in Kuwait. Where the summer is scorching hot. It all began with growing tomato when I found that it survived then I immersed myself with all that is gardening. I fell on love with growing my own food, composting, and enjoying the sunshine in Kuwait. Now I give lectures and plan events to introduce gardening to the masses in Kuwait. As a result I started selling raised beds and organic compost that I make myself. Now you have the chance to start your own garden.
